latte threshold
A singularity in the partial derivative of attention with respect to monetary cost. The point at which something is sufficiently expensive to be worth considering for more than a few seconds (leading to an involved cost-benefit analysis). Anecdotal evidence suggests that this varies in relation to the pricing of beverages at Starbucks.
